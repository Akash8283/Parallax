ABOUT THE PARALLX EFFECT

The parallax effect is a modern web design technique that creates an illusion of depth and movement by making background elements move at a slower pace than the foreground content as you scroll. Inspired by natural human perception, it adds a 3D-like experience to flat screens, making websites feel more interactive and immersive. By layering images, videos, or text at different speeds, the parallax effect enhances storytelling, keeps users engaged, and gives your site a sleek, futuristic vibe.
